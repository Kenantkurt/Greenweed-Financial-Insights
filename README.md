This project focuses on analyzing **Greenweed Company’s operational and financial data for October 2021**. The goal was to extract key insights and assess profitability, costs, and advertising performance.

## What I Did
- Collected and cleaned Greenweed’s daily operational and financial data
- Calculated **daily and total revenues, costs, and margins**
- Derived **average gross and operational margins** to understand profitability trends
- Analyzed **advertising performance and total order volumes**
- Identified actionable insights for strategic decision-making

## How I Did It
- Tools used: **Google Sheets, Excel**
- Applied formulas and aggregations to summarize large datasets
- Calculated total and range margins, average gross and operational margins, and trends over time

## Conclusion

This project highlights how operational and financial data can be transformed into clear, actionable insights.  
The analysis shows strong profitability, meaningful advertising contributions, and clear trends across daily performance.  
These findings support better planning around costs, marketing efficiency, and revenue optimization.


- Revenue and operational activity showed consistent daily performance with noticeable fluctuations.
- Profitability remained solid, with both gross and operational margins maintaining healthy levels.
- Advertising and order volumes contributed meaningfully to overall margins, highlighting the importance of marketing efficiency.
- Trends observed in costs, revenue, and margins provide actionable insights for optimizing operations and strategic decision-making.
- Overall, the project demonstrates how raw operational data can be transformed into meaningful business insights.

## Skills Demonstrated

- Advanced Excel calculations (SUMIFS, AVERAGEIFS, XLOOKUP, IF logic)
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Financial KPI analysis (gross margin, operational margin, MoM trends)
- Pivot tables & data summarization
- Revenue, cost, and profitability analysis
- Google Sheets for cloud-based data collaboration
